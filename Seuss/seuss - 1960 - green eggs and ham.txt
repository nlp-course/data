# Green Eggs and Ham
## (By Doctor Seuss) 

SAM	I am Sam.
SAM	I am Sam.
SAM	Sam I am.

GUY	That Sam-I-am! 
GUY	That Sam-I-am! 
GUY	I do not like that Sam-I-am!

SAM	Do you like green eggs and ham?

GUY	I do not like them, Sam-I-am.
GUY	I do not like green eggs and ham.

SAM	Would you like them here or there?

GUY	I would not like them here or there.
GUY	I would not like them anywhere.
GUY	I do not like green eggs and ham.
GUY	I do not like them, Sam-I-am.

SAM	Would you like them in a house?
SAM	Would you like them with a mouse?

GUY	I do not like them in a house.
GUY	I do not like them with a mouse.
GUY	I do not like them here or there.
GUY	I do not like them anywhere.
GUY	I do not like green eggs and ham.
GUY	I do not like them, Sam-I-am.

SAM	Would you eat them in a box?
SAM	Would you eat them with a fox?

GUY	Not in a box. 
GUY	Not with a fox.
GUY	Not in a house. 
GUY	Not with a mouse.
GUY	I would not eat them here or there.
GUY	I would not eat them anywhere.
GUY	I would not eat green eggs and ham.
GUY	I do not like them, Sam-I-am.

SAM	Would you?
SAM	Could you?
SAM	In a car?
SAM	Eat them!
SAM	Eat them!
SAM	Here they are.

GUY	I would not, could not, in a car.

SAM	You may like them.
SAM	You will see.
SAM	You may like them in a tree!

GUY	I would not, could not in a tree.
GUY	Not in a car!
GUY	You let me be.
GUY	I do not like them in a box.
GUY	I do not like them with a fox.
GUY	I do not like them in a house.
GUY	I do not like them with a mouse.
GUY	I do not like them here or there.
GUY	I do not like them anywhere.
GUY	I do not like green eggs and ham.
GUY	I do not like them, Sam-I-am.

SAM	A train!
SAM	A train!
SAM	A train!
SAM	A train!
SAM	Could you, would you, on a train?

GUY	Not on a train!
GUY	Not in a tree!
GUY	Not in a car!
GUY	Sam!
GUY	Let me be!
GUY	I would not, could not, in a box.
GUY	I would not, could not, with a fox.
GUY	I will not eat them with a mouse.
GUY	I will not eat them in a house.
GUY	I will not eat them here or there.
GUY	I will not eat them anywhere.
GUY	I do not eat green eggs and ham.
GUY	I do not like them, Sam-I-am.

SAM	Say! 
SAM	In the dark? 
SAM	Here in the dark!
SAM	Would you, could you, in the dark?

GUY	I would not, could not, in the dark.

SAM	Would you, could you, in the rain?

GUY	I would not, could not in the rain.
GUY	Not in the dark.
GUY	Not on a train.
GUY	Not in a car.
GUY	Not in a tree.
GUY	I do not like them, Sam, you see.
GUY	Not in a house.
GUY	Not in a box.
GUY	Not with a mouse.
GUY	Not with a fox.
GUY	I will not eat them here or there.
GUY	I do not like them anywhere!

SAM	You do not like green eggs and ham?

GUY	I do not like them, Sam-I-am.

SAM	Could you, would you, with a goat?

GUY	I would not, could not with a goat!

SAM	Would you, could you, on a boat?

GUY	I could not, would not, on a boat.
GUY	I will not, will not, with a goat.
GUY	I will not eat them in the rain.
GUY	I will not eat them on a train.
GUY	Not in the dark!
GUY	Not in a tree!
GUY	Not in a car!
GUY	You let me be!
GUY	I do not like them in a box.
GUY	I do not like them with a fox.
GUY	I will not eat them in a house.
GUY	I do not like them with a mouse.
GUY	I do not like them here or there.
GUY	I do not like them ANYWHERE!
GUY	I do not like green eggs and ham!
GUY	I do not like them, Sam-I-am.

SAM	You do not like them. 
SAM	So you say.
SAM	Try them!
SAM	Try them! 
SAM	And you may.
SAM	Try them and you may, I say.

GUY	Sam! 
GUY	If you will let me be, I will try them. 
GUY	You will see.
GUY	Say! 
GUY	I like green eggs and ham!
GUY	I do!
GUY	I like them, Sam-I-am!
GUY	And I would eat them in a boat.
GUY	And I would eat them with a goat...
GUY	And I will eat them, in the rain.
GUY	And in the dark. And on a train.
GUY	And in a car. And in a tree.
GUY	They are so good, so good, you see!
GUY	So I will eat them in a box.
GUY	And I will eat them with a fox.
GUY	And I will eat them in a house.
GUY	And I will eat them with a mouse.
GUY	And I will eat them here and there.
GUY	Say!
GUY	I will eat them ANYWHERE!
GUY	I do so like green eggs and ham!
GUY	Thank you! 
GUY	Thank you, Sam-I-am.
